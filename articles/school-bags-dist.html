School Bags Distribution in Agarkar Night School


Brand Ambassador of Masoom and Bollywood star Mr Ashish Chowdhry visited Agarkar and BVM – Worli night school on the occasion of his twin daughters’ Salara and Sammah’s first birthday. Some of his Twamily (Purnima, Priya, Kiran, Saru, Kritika, Anu, Anjali) donated bags to night school students.

Few more Twamily members (Anusuya, Charu, Leelu, Melanie and Uma) of Mr. Ashish donated 20000/- purchase of Mobile Science Lab or laptop.

The head masters of Agarkar and BVM schools Mr Anantkumar Patil and Mataprasad Mishra thanked Masoom and Ashish Chowdhry for his support

Savita Sartape – Program Manager at Masoom spoke on behalf of Masoom. Ashish Chowdhry addressed the students about the importance of the education. He added , “We are not real heros. You are real heros who inspite of hardships works in a day time & attend the school in the night. Only study can take to you to the level where we are now. Just study hard and people like us are always there to help you.” He also thanked his Twamily / his fans and said “I am overwhelmed by the love he receives from them. The blessings my children got are because of all of you”.
