What we do

 

 

Education Infrastructure Building

This component includes providing textbooks, notebooks, workbooks, science mobile laboratory, math-kits, extra-tuition classes and special moderator sessions for students appearing for SSC examination, and nutritional support etc.

 

Capacity Building

This component has training workshops for teachers and students, personalized counseling, English conversation classes, field visits, home visits, vocational guidance.

Career Cell

Masoom has set up a career cell in 2011-12 in order to provide vocational education & employment opportunities to night school students.



Advocacy

Advocacy is a very important component of the program. Masoom will try to get the government to take more responsibility of the night schools.
